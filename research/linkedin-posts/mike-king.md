## 1. Measurement Is the First Thing Generative AI Broke

**Post URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/michaelkingphilly_measurement-is-the-first-thing-generative-activity-7472586056807272452-nlsd  
**Date:** 2026-06-16

**Summary:**  
Mike argues that generative AI has broken the old SEO measurement model. Rankings and traffic worked when search returned a list of links, but AI Search fans out the query, synthesizes a single answer, and rewards the citation the model trusts rather than the page that ranks first.

**Key Insight:**  
AI Search behaves more like a brand channel than a classic performance channel. Teams need a new scoreboard that tracks citations, trust, and influence rather than only rankings and clicks.

**Why It Matters:**  
This is central to AI-powered SEO content production because content teams need to measure whether their assets are being selected, synthesized, and cited by AI systems. Old dashboards can make a winning AI visibility strategy look like a traffic decline.

**Date:** 2026-05-26

**Summary:**
