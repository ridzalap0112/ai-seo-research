## Expert Background

Ross Simmonds is the founder and CEO of Foundation Marketing and Distribution.ai. He is one of the strongest experts for this repository because he treats content performance as a distribution and influence problem, not only an SEO ranking problem.

## Core SEO Philosophy

Ross's SEO philosophy is distribution-first. He argues that content teams should research the audience, create content worth discussing, distribute it aggressively, and optimize it for each channel. This is especially relevant as buyers discover and evaluate brands across search engines, YouTube, communities, social platforms, and AI assistants.

## AI Search Perspective

Ross frames AI search as part of a broader shift from traffic to influence. If buyers research inside ChatGPT, Gemini, and other assistants, the brand may influence demand without receiving a clean click path. This changes measurement, content goals, and distribution strategy.

He also emphasizes YouTube as a B2B search surface. Video content and transcripts can become discovery assets for classic search, AI answers, and buyer education.

## Content Production Frameworks

- Research the audience and industry before producing content.
- Create content that deserves discussion and citation.
- Distribute content across LinkedIn, YouTube, communities, newsletters, and partner ecosystems.
- Optimize each asset for the channel where it will travel.
- Measure influence signals alongside traffic and pipeline.
