## Expert Background

Ryan Law is Director of Content Marketing at Ahrefs and previously held senior content roles at Animalz. He is one of the strongest practitioners for this repository because he combines editorial judgment, SEO strategy, content operations, and hands-on AI workflow experimentation.

## Core SEO Philosophy

Ryan's philosophy centers on usefulness, strategic differentiation, and operational leverage. He does not frame content as a volume game alone. Instead, he focuses on whether content has a reason to exist, whether it is materially better or different, and whether the production system supports quality at speed.

## AI Search Perspective

Ryan is skeptical of shallow AI-content debates. His framing is that "does AI content work?" is the wrong question. The better question is how AI content differs from traditional content and what strategy those differences require.

He also brings evidence discipline to emerging AI-search tactics. His llms.txt research shows that popular AI optimization ideas can spread before they are supported by bot behavior or measurable adoption.

## Content Production Frameworks

- Use AI to accelerate research, outlining, drafting, and editing while preserving human editorial judgment.
- Build content systems that are automation-ready: static site generators, GitHub workflows, Netlify or Cloudflare Pages, and MCP-style connectors.
- Treat keyword research as one input into a broader production workflow.
- Evaluate AI content by differentiation, strategic fit, and quality control.
- Validate AI-search tactics with evidence before operationalizing them.
