## Expert Background

Wil Reynolds is the founder of Seer Interactive and a long-time SEO, analytics, and digital strategy practitioner. He is valuable for this repository because he connects search strategy with data infrastructure, paid media, customer intent, and organizational adoption.

## Core SEO Philosophy

Wil's philosophy is grounded in data, curiosity, and customer understanding. He tends to challenge tool-first thinking and pushes teams to improve the questions they ask. In AI search, this shows up clearly in his emphasis on prompt quality before vendor selection.

## AI Search Perspective

Wil's strongest current AI-search insight is that prompt tracking quality matters more than which prompt tracking vendor is selected. If the prompt set is weak, the visibility dashboard will be weak. This perspective is highly useful because many AI-search teams are buying tools before defining what they need to measure.

He also frames AI adoption as an organizational behavior shift. Speed is only valuable when teams trust the process and understand how to evaluate outputs.

## Content Production Frameworks

- Define high-quality prompt sets before buying or judging AI visibility platforms.
- Use customer research and search intent data to decide which prompts matter.
- Connect SEO and paid data to understand changing intent patterns.
- Build trust into AI workflows through QA, review, and transparent measurement.
- Prioritize adoption behaviors, not just tool access.
