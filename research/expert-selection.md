# Expert Selection: AI-Powered SEO Content Production

Date updated: 2026-06-19

## Ranking Criteria

Experts were selected for demonstrated implementation experience in SEO, AI search, content production, growth strategy, analytics, or content operations. The goal was to prioritize operators and practitioners over generic AI commentators.

Evaluation criteria:

- Direct relevance to AI Search, GEO, AEO, LLM visibility, Relevance Engineering, or AI-assisted content production
- Evidence of real-world implementation through agency work, consulting, SaaS content leadership, product development, or research
- Active public publishing on LinkedIn, YouTube, newsletters, blogs, podcasts, webinars, or conference talks
- Ability to contribute distinct frameworks, workflows, measurement models, or tactical examples
- Usefulness for B2B SaaS teams adapting SEO content production for AI-mediated discovery
